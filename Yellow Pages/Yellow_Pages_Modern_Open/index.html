about

Global Yellow Pages (GYP) has always been committed to enabling SMEs. As such, GYP's stable of products aims to provide clients with the right tools and solutions for their search and advertising needs. Having pioneered search solutions in Singapore with the release of its first telephone directory in 1968, GYP's unrivalled information database has been supporting businesses for over 40 years.
